Thank you for playing this game, which is in open beta. If you have any comments or problems, please click on the ? symbol on the bottom right of the screen. You will be able to enter your comment which will be sent to me.

Features: 3 different size planes enter from the left Planes have passengers, which count towards the Players score Planes have fuel, which is consumed as they fly. When planes run out of fuel they crash Planes can collide in mid-air Planes can be landed at runways by clicking on the plane, and then clicking on a runway that can handle the size of the plane Runways can only handle one plane at a time. Two game modes, Time Trial and Perfection Option to submit your score and view the top scores
